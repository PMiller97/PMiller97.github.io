About Me

Hello, my name is Paige. I am a Software Developer, residing in Canton, Ohio. I've been in retail for the past 4 years, up until my store closed. At that point in time, I decided I needed to get a start on my career. I've always loved designing, which in my retail experience would have been the visualizing. My brother-in-law is a Database Administrator, and for years he would show me the websites he could create. I always thought what he could was so cool, and he was my entire inspiration in my interst in developing. I was so excited to find out about this amazing oppurtunity to become a junior developer through WeCanCodeIt's program. I have acquired so many skills I never thought possible to learn in such a short period. Skills including C#, .NET, ASP.net, MVC, HTML, JavaScript, JQuery, Bootstrap, and CSS.
